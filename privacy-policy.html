Privacy Policy - Safe Gallery

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Safe Gallery ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Access

Safe Gallery requires access to your device's photo and video gallery (storage/media permissions) in order to provide its core features: viewing, organizing, hiding, and recovering photos and videos. This data is accessed and processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Data Storage

All your photos, videos, hidden albums, and recycle bin contents are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal media files on any external server.

3. Advertising

Safe Gallery displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data such as advertising ID, device information, and usage data to show relevant ads. This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy, available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings.

4. PIN / Hidden Album Security

Any PIN you set for the Hidden Album feature is stored securely on your device only and is never transmitted or shared with us or any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

Aside from Google AdMob for advertisements, Safe Gallery does not share your data with any third-party service.

6. Children's Privacy

Safe Gallery does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
